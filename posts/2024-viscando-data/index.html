Dataset: Trajectories of interacting drivers and pedestrians

Link: https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/ZBFXU

Data from the real-world data collection described in (Kalantari et al., 2025), using a setup of two stereo camera sensors to record road user trajectories at two locations in Leeds, UK, with frequent driver-pedestrian interactions, one week per location.

Kalantari, A. H., Lin, Y. S., Mohammadi, A., Merat, N., & Markkula, G. (2025). Testing the validity of multi-participant distributed simulation for understanding and modeling road user interaction. PsyArXiv preprint. https://doi.org/10.31234/osf.io/gk9af
