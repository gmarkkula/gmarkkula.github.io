A recipe for pain-free(?) academic writing

Judging by most reports on the subject, for most people most of the time, writing is a painful experience. However, if what you are writing is something like a paper or a thesis, you are in luck: I think academic writing can be an exception to the universal rule of more writing = more pain, and here I will outline a recipe for how most of the pain can be taken out of it, by iterative top-down writing.
